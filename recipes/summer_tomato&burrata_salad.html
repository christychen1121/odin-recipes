Summer Tomato & Burrata Salad

Description

This recipe is a good choice for an appetizer and is suitable for a summer day!

Ingredients

  • One burrata cheese (about 100 gr)
  • 50 gr cherry tomato, halved
  • 5 gr tomato salad seasoning
  • 5 gr chopped garlic
  • 30 gr wild arugula
  • A few basil leaves
  • 30 ml EVOO
  • To taste, salt & black pepper

Method

  1. Place in a mixing bowl, the cherry tomato, the seasoning, basil, garlic, evoo, salt and black pepper.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Place the burrata on a serving plate and top with the cherry tomato salad.
  4. Sprinkle with some more seasoning top with arugula. Serve immediately.
