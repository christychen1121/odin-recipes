Pollo Alla Cacciatore

Description

This recipe is a main course. It's very juicy and flavourful!

Ingredients

  • 100 gr chicken parts
  • 50 gr white onion, small diced
  • 50 gr celery, small diced
  • 30 gr carrots, small diced
  • 30 ml red wine
  • 4 gr garlic, chopped
  • 100 gr peeled tomatoes
  • 50 ml EVOO
  • A small bunch rosemary
  • To taste, salt & black pepper

Method

  1. Season the chicken parts with salt, pepper.
  2. Place over medium heat a small size sauce pot, add EVOO and bring to a hot temperature.
  3. Add the chicken parts and pan fry from the skin side first.
  4. Cook for about 3 minutes each side.
  5. Add the onion, carrots, celery. Cook for 5 minutes stirring continuously until the vegetables become translucent.
  6. Add garlic and herbs. Stir continuously.
  7. Add the wine and let evaporate.
  8. Add the peeled tomato, season to taste with salt & black pepper.
  9. Cook for 30 minutes or until done.
  10. If the liquid reduces during the cooking process, feel free to add some water or broth.
  11. When ready, remove from the fire and serve immediately.
