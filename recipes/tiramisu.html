Tiramisu

Description

This recipe is a dessert that everyone loves. It's delightful to have a tiramisu after dinner!

Ingredients

  • 500 gr mascarpone
  • 300 gr savoiardi cookies (lady fingers)
  • 150 gr sugar
  • Six egg yolks
  • 10 ml marsala wine or Baileys (optional)
  • 50 ml espresso coffee
  • For dusting, cocoa powder

Method

  1. Place the egg yolk, sugar and marsala wine in the "kitchen aid" bowl with the wisk attachment, start to whip until soft and fluffy.
  2. When ready fold in the mascarpone until well incorporate and smooth.
  3. Soak the savoiardi (quickly) in the coffee and place them in the appropriate cake tray.
  4. Add on top the mascarpone mixture and lay evenly.
  5. Repeat the process two more times.
  6. When done dust the surface with cocoa powder and serve to your guests.
